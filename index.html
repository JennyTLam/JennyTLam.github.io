Hi, I’m Jenny.

I’m a fourth-year student at Northwestern University pursuing the BS/MS program in Computer Science and the Segal Design Certificate.

I have a perpetual curiosity and a constant strive to learn new things. As a computer scientist and designer, I love combining the logical and meticulous problem-solving aspect of coding with the creative, empathy-driven process of interface design. I also love to help others learn, and have served as a computer science peer mentor, senior facilitator for first-year engineering peer-guided study groups, tutor with NU|Tutors, and Design Lead for .dev, a software development-focused student organization on campus. As long as you’re willing to try new things, I can help you through it!

If I’m not working on a new project, you might find me assembling the final pieces of a 3D modular origami figure, cooking, or learning a new language (programming or otherwise).
