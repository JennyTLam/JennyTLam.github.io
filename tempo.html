Tempo

Students learning remotely have difficulty finding a clear distinction between work and home. To motivate students to study, Tempo is an app that promotes effective study habits using the pomodoro technique. As users complete work cycles, their fish tanks grow lively with more fish. After login, users start a pomodoro timer (4-25 minute work, 3-5 minute break cycles) to pace their studying. After each work cycle, users are rewarded with a fish added to their tank. Users can track their progress against their friends using the Leaderboard where they can view friend fish tanks as well as send messages and fish food gifts.
