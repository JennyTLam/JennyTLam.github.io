Work2Gather

Work2Gather is a webapp that partners users to maintain accountability and track your progress towards shared goals.

SKILLS

App Design, React Web Development, Agile

PROJECT

COMP_SCI 394: Agile Software Development Course Project

TEAMMATES

George Malty, Pravika Joshi, Fenghang Yao, Yutao Wu, Jiezhuo Lyu

People have difficulty staying motivated amidst COVID-19 quarantining and social distancing restrictions. Work2Gather helps increase motivation by breaking down goals into day by day habits and sharing progress with friends for mutual motivation.


Quickly and easily assess and track daily progress towards goals

Work2Gather's dashboard displays goal cards with a progress circle that indicates progress for both the user and their friend. Circle size indicates when the user's daily goal is in progress, met, or exceeded.


Visualize short-term and long-term progress based on user preferences

The "See More" tab allows users to view charts of their progress over time in the form of a bar, line, or area chart.


Achieve goals through partner reminders and mutual motivation

Users can alert their partners when they have completed their goal to motivate them to start making progress for their own goal circle. Once completed, users can archive goal cards as a record of their accomplishment!
